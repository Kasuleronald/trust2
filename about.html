About Trust Construction Company

Your trusted source for quality construction materials in Kigali

Our Story

Founded in 2022, Trust Construction Company has grown to become one of the leading suppliers of construction materials in Kigali. We have expanded to serve contractors, builders, and homeowners across the city and the entire Country.

We take pride in sourcing only the highest quality materials from trusted manufacturers, ensuring that every project built with our supplies meets the highest standards of durability and safety.

Our Mission

To provide quality construction materials at competitive prices, delivered with exceptional customer service. We believe that quality materials are the foundation of every great building project.

Our Location

Visit our showrooms at Umukundo Center, Gisozi, Kigali and at Chez John Building in Kicukiro, where our knowledgeable staff can help you select the right materials for your project. We offer on-site consultations and can arrange delivery to your construction site.
